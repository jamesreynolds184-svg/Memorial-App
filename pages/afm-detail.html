    About the Armed Forces Memorial

    The Armed Forces Memorial at the National Memorial Arboretum commemorates the men and women of the Armed Forces who have given their lives in service to their country since the Second World War.

    The Memorial consists of a series of bronze panels displaying the names of over 16,000 service personnel who have died in operations or training since 1945.
